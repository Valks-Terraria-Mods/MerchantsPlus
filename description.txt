You can think of this as a merchant overhaul mod. The mod is 100% compatible with multiplayer / singleplayer. The following mechanics listed below heavily effect all vanilla and custom merchants.

[c/ffb2fd:Items]
+ Sell more items based on the bosses / enemies defeated.
+ Sell items based on your characters build.
+ Have a chance to drop items on death.

[c/ffb2fd:Dialog]
+ World conditions effect how the dialog is played out.
+ Mostly humerous personalities.
+ Some dialog may reward you with items.
+ Interactions with other merchants.

[c/ffb2fd:Stats]
+ 300 health for all merchants.
+ Slightly reduced sizes.
+ Shoot different projectiles based on bosses defeated.

[c/ffb2fd:Msc]
+ All merchants have the cycle shop mechanic.
+ The Angler, Guide, Nurse, Tax Collector and Old Man are now merchants.
+ All vanilla merchant items have been moved around.
+ The way merchants arrive to your town is changed. They try to arrive every 10 seconds.
+ Merchants do not require houses to join your town.
+ Truffle no longer needs to be in mushroom biome.
+ Santa Claus comes even if it isn't christmas.
+ You no longer have to find the mechanic, wizard, tinkerer, tax collector, or stylist.. they automatically come to your town.
+ The skeleton and travelling merchant move into your town in hardmode.

[c/ffb2fd:Mods Supported!]
+ Terraria Overhaul
